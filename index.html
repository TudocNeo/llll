Hello Welcome to the webpage of our Outreach Project, "A Day of Kindness And Compassion."

Objectives and Goals

Helping those in Need

To donate rags, old shirt and old fabrics, so that the church can repurpose them into garments and goods made from cloth.

Encouraging Spiritual Development

To implement a recognition-based program to show our gratitude to church administration/servers and contribution workers.

Advancing Social Progress

To create a successful curriculum that centers on the concepts of God and the Catholic Church.
